# FCI question inputs — not redistributed

Each unconstrained generation in this folder was produced from a Force Concept
Inventory (FCI) question as input (question image/screenshot). **The FCI question
images are third-party assessment material and are not redistributed in this
archive** (the repository owner does not hold redistribution rights). See
`../THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md`.

The question numbers and scenario descriptions are reported below for reference;
these do not reproduce the FCI items themselves. To obtain the FCI, see the
original publication cited in `../THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md`.

| Folder | FCI item | Manuscript figure | Scenario (paraphrase) |
|--------|----------|-------------------|-----------------------|
| `Q05/` | FCI Q5 | Figure 1 | A ball thrown straight up (free fall, no air resistance) |
| `Q16/` | FCI Q16 | Figure 2 | A ball launched horizontally off an elevated ledge (projectile) |
| `Q17/` | FCI Q17 | Figure 3 | An object dropped from a single-story roof (free fall) |
| `Q22/` | FCI Q22 | Figure 4 | A ball driven down a fairway (projectile, with air resistance) |
| `Q23/` | FCI Q23 | Figure 5 | An object released horizontally from a flying airliner (projectile) |

The simulations run without these images — the images were model inputs, not
assets used by the generated pages.
